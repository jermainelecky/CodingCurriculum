Colors of the Rainbow

What are the Colors of the Rainbow

  • Red
  • Orange
  • Yellow
  • Green
  • Blue
  • Indigo
  • Violet

Every rainbow you see will have these seven colors in this order. The easiest way to remember the rainbow color order is to use the acronym ROYGBIV, in which each letter stands for the first letter of the color names

What is a Rainbow

Isaac Newton discovered that when clear white light passes through a prism, it refracts into different colors in a particular order, or what we know as a rainbow
